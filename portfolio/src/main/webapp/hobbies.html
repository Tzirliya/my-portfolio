Hobbies


Since I was nine years old, I've taken piano lessons for most years of my life. Less than two years ago, I bought a ukulele and violin and proceded to teach it to myself. Now I love practicing all three instruments as a hobby. Recently, I've tried to compose my own music. Classical music inspires me most, as that's what I love listening to.


Around two years ago, I decided that I needed to learn how to cook. I began with frying because I was able to see the food cooking in front of me, so I knew that I wouldn't undercook or burn anything. I then branched off into other forms of cooking and I even dabbled in baking. Now I enjoy making all types of food. My favorite part is eating it!

I've always enjoyed going for long walks. One summer, I went to a three-week hiking and camping camp where we lived in tents, hiked many trails, and had lengthy bus journeys along the Rocky Mountains in the US. After high school, I took a gap year in Israel where I went on many hikes. My favorite hike was one where I walked down a mountain, passed some streams of water, and climbed up a nearby mountain into a neighboring city. I did that hike five times! Currently, I live in New York, and sadly there aren't too many hikes nearby, so I enjoy walking in parks instead.

When I was a child, my mom bought me a Rubik's cube and handed me a book that included the algorithms to solve it. I promptly memorized the steps and was able to solve it from memory. Years later, I forgot the algorithms until a friend taught them to me. Now I love to solve all different types of Rubik's cubes. They range in different sizes and shapes. Over the past few years, my collection has grown to over ten different types of cubes.
